Connect at Gallaudet University

Virtual Undergraduate Open Houses

With our Virtual Undergraduate Open Houses, we bring Gallaudet University to you! Learn about our general studies program and our five academic schools. With over 40 majors and minors, many student organizations, and a thriving athletics program, there truly is something for everyone at Gallaudet! The Open House program will cover our Career Center, academic success support programs, and Residence Life and Housing, as well as the application process. Sign up today!

Weekly Information Sessions

We hold Information sessions every week. These sessions, conducted by our admissions counselors and students ambassadors, are an opportunity to learn more about the Gallaudet University experience, including academic programs, academic advising, internships, residential life, extracurricular activities, financial aid, admission requirements, and other topics.

Special Topic Webinars

Undergraduate Admissions offers hour-long webinars and question-and-answer sessions on essential topics. Some recent examples include Financial Aid, Preparing for College, Transferring to Gallaudet, and Onboarding. Watch for announcements throughout the academic year!

Summer Youth Competitions & Webinars

We offer a variety of competitions and webinars for middle school and high school students throughout the summer. Here are our recent offerings:

  • Debate
  • Got Skills?
  • Immerse into ASL
  • Deaf Youth Pride
  • Summer Spotlight Webinar Series
  • Deaf Biz
Meet us online and explore a world of possibilities! For more information please visit Youth Programs.
